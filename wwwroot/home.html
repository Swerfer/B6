How B6 Works

Connect & Fund. Use a Cronos-compatible wallet with a little CRO for gas and the entry fee.

Pick a mission. Join during the enrollment window; each mission shows fee, duration, rounds, and player limits.

Mission starts. When the timer hits zero, the game enters 'Mission Time'—this is when round payouts can be claimed.

Bank a round. Press Bank at the right moment, before another player does, to claim that round’s share of the CRO pool.

Cooldown. After each win there’s a brief pause; watch the countdown and be ready for the next window.

Finish. The mission ends when its scheduled end time is reached or all rounds are completed. Results and payouts are recorded on-chain for everyone to see.
